How to Delete Your Data

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from Meet App, you can do so using one of the methods below.

Option 1: Delete Through the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Meet app on your device
  2. Go to your Profile tab
  3. Tap on Settings
  4. Scroll down and tap on Delete Account or Account Settings
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm account deletion
  6. Enter your password or confirm deletion when prompted

Note: This will immediately delete your account and all associated data.

Option 2: Contact Us via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to delete your account via email:

  1. Send an email to: djtomasevic98@gmail.com
  2. Subject line: "Delete My Account"
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your username or display name
    • Confirmation that you want to delete your account
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we will permanently remove:

  • Your Profile: All profile information, photos, bio, and preferences
  • Your Messages: All chat messages and conversations
  • Your Matches: All matches and encounter records
  • Your Location Data: All stored location history
  • Your Account Data: Email, authentication data, and account settings
  • Your Activity: Likes, views, and other interactions

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Permanent Deletion: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Data Retention: Some information may be retained for legal or security purposes (e.g., transaction records, fraud prevention) for up to 90 days
  • Third-Party Services: Data shared with third-party services (e.g., Firebase, hosting providers) will be deleted according to their policies
  • Backup Data: Deleted data may exist in backups for up to 30 days before being permanently removed

What Happens After Deletion

  • You will be logged out of the app immediately
  • Your profile will no longer be visible to other users
  • You will stop receiving notifications
  • You will not be able to recover any deleted data
  • You can create a new account with the same email address after deletion

Data Deletion Timeline

  • Immediate: Account access disabled, profile hidden from other users
  • Within 24 hours: Most user data deleted from active databases
  • Within 30 days: Data removed from backup systems
  • Within 90 days: All data permanently deleted (except legally required records)
