Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, age, gender, and profile information
  • Location Data: Your current location (with your explicit permission) to find nearby users
  • Profile Data: Photos, bio, interests, preferences, and other details you choose to share
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including matches, messages, and encounters

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our matching and location-based services
  • Match you with nearby users based on your preferences
  • Send you notifications about matches, messages, and encounters
  • Ensure the safety and security of our platform
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

3. Location Data

We collect your location data only when you explicitly enable location tracking in the app. This data is used solely to:

  • Find and match you with nearby users
  • Display your location on the map (only to matched users)

You can disable location tracking at any time in the app settings. When disabled, we stop collecting location data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your profile information is visible to other users based on your privacy settings
  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party services that help us operate the app (e.g., Firebase, hosting services)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication methods
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data (see Data Deletion instructions)
  • Opt-out of location tracking
  • Control your privacy settings in the app

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
